Artificial Intelligence is going to dominate software engineering for several reasons. Firstly, AI has the ability to analyze large amounts of data in a short amount of time, which can help speed up the development process. Secondly, AI can detect patterns and relationships in data that would be difficult or impossible for humans to detect. This ability can assist developers in identifying and resolving issues quickly. Thirdly, AI can learn from experience and make adjustments on its own, improving its own performance in the development process. Lastly, AI can be integrated with other emerging technologies such as automated testing and deployment, which can greatly enhance the efficiency and accuracy of software engineering operations. As AI continues to develop and advance, it is likely that it will become an indispensable tool in the software engineering industry.
